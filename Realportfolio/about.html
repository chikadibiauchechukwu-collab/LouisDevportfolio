About Me

The man behind the code

Hi, I'm Louis Dev

Based in Rivers State, Nigeria, I've been crafting digital products for over 2+ years. What started as a fascination with how websites work turned into a career building them for startups, agencies, and global brands.

I specialize in modern frontend technologies – React, Next.js, TypeScript – but my real passion lies in the intersection of design and engineering. I believe the best products come from truly understanding both the user and the code.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me contributing to open‑source, mentoring aspiring developers, or exploring the latest in AI and interactive design.
