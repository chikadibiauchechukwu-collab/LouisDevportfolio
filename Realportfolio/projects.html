Louis Mart is a modern luxury sneaker e-commerce website that showcases premium footwear collections, allowing customers to browse, search, and purchase stylish sneakers through an elegant, user-friendly shopping experience. The platform features product catalogs, detailed shoe displays, smooth animations, secure shopping functionality, and a premium design focused on fashion, comfort, and convenience.

Alpine‑crest

Alpine Crest Capital is a Switzerland-based investment firm that acquires and grows software businesses, SaaS companies, mobile apps, and other digital ventures. The website allows business owners to estimate their company's value, submit their business for acquisition, download an NDA, and contact the firm for potential sale opportunities. It is essentially a professional platform for buying and scaling technology businesses.
