My Path

The road to becoming a developer

I wasn't born writing code. I learned through mentorship, persistence, and a deep passion for building things.

2023

The spark of curiosity

I started tinkering with HTML and CSS after seeing a friend's personal website. It felt like magic – I wanted to create that same feeling for others.

2025

Mentored by a frontend engineer named Cisco

I was lucky to find a senior frontend engineer who took me under his wing. He taught me JavaScript fundamentals, Git workflows, and how to debug like a pro. More importantly, he showed me how to think like an engineer.

2022

Learning design from a UI/UX expert

I crossed paths with a talented UI/UX designer who opened my eyes to the world of design systems, user research, and prototyping. She taught me how to see interfaces from the user's perspective – a skill that transformed my development work.

2026

Diving into frameworks

After getting comfortable with vanilla JavaScript, I dove headfirst into React. The component model clicked instantly. From there I explored Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS – each one adding a new layer to my stack.

Pesent – Present

UI Engineer & beyond

Today I build production‑grade applications with React, Next.js, Node.js, and TypeScript. I've worked with startups, agencies, and global brands. And I'm still learning every day – because the journey never really ends.
